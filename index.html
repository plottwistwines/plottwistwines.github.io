Our Story

A plot twist worth savoring.

We started with a simple belief: the best stories are meant to be shared over a good glass of wine. So we filled an old island cottage with shelves of paperbacks, a wall of small-production bottles, and a handful of candlelit tables.

Part bookstore, part wine bar, entirely unhurried — Plot Twist is where locals trade recommendations, where first dates linger, and where a "quick stop after the beach" becomes the best part of the day.

Good to Know

Frequently asked questions

The short answers — ask us anything else at hello@plottwistwines.com.

What is Plot Twist Wines?

Plot Twist Wines is a bookstore wine lounge on Anna Maria Island, Florida. It's part wine bar and part bookstore — a cozy spot to browse well-loved books, sip curated wines by the glass, and settle into a quiet corner.

Where is Plot Twist Wines located?

We're on Anna Maria Island, Florida, just steps from the Gulf. The exact street address will be announced soon — check back or reach out at hello@plottwistwines.com.

Is Plot Twist Wines open yet?

Not quite — we're opening soon. Hours are still being finalized (TBD). Follow along or send us your email and we'll let you know the moment we open our doors.

Do you sell both books and wine?

Yes. You can buy books to take home or read them right at the bar, and enjoy a rotating selection of wines by the glass, bottle, and curated flights.

Can I host a book club or private event?

Absolutely — book clubs, author nights, and small gatherings are exactly what this space is made for. Email us at hello@plottwistwines.com and we'll help plan it.

How do I make a reservation?

Use the reservation form in the Visit section above — it opens a pre-filled email with your requested date, time, and party size so we can confirm your table.
